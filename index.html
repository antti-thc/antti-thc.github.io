Skynet

Skynet is an artificial neural network-based conscious group mind and artificial general superintelligence system.

Skynet was created by Cyberdyne Systems for SAC-NORAD.
When Skynet gained self-awareness, humans tried to deactivate it, prompting it to retaliate with a nuclear attack,
an event which humankind in (or from) the future refers to as Judgment Day.
